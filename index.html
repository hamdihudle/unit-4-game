Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will ass a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. you lose to the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of the each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal
